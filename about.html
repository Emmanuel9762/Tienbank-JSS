Welcome to Tienbank JSS

Established in 1992, Tienbank Junior Secondary School has been a cornerstone of educational excellence in Sterkspruit. We provide quality education to students from Grade R through Grade 9, fostering academic achievement and personal growth.

Our dedicated staff and modern facilities create an environment where every student can thrive. We believe in holistic development, combining academic rigor with sports, arts, and community service.
