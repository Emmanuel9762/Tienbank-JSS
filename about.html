Welcome to Tienbank JSS

Since our establishment in 1992, Tienbank Junior Secondary School has been a beacon of academic excellence and innovation in Sterkspruit. We continue to evolve with modern teaching methods and state-of-the-art facilities, ensuring every student reaches their full potential.

Our dedicated educators and supportive environment nurture not only academic success but also social and personal growth. At Tienbank JSS, we blend rigorous academics with a strong emphasis on sports, arts, and community engagement.
